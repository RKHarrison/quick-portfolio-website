002: NC News: Full-stack responsive web app.

//Javascript / Express.js & React.js / PostgresSQL / Jest & Supertest//

Leveraging responsive, mobile first design, this online news content management web application was built over 2 weeks as part of a 13-week digital skills bootcamp with Northcoders. Logged-in users can view, rate and comment on paginated news articles sorted by different queries.

The backend was built in Express.js and PostgresSQL, utilising Jest and Supertest for Test Driven Development, demonstrating use of MVC architecture and principles such as reusable, DRY code.

The frontend is a React.js Single Page Application, demonstrating understanding of state, context, custom hooks and principles such as optimistic rendering.
